# Офферы и переговоры

> **TL;DR:** Оффер — не конец процесса, а начало negotiation. 70% кандидатов negotiate. Будь готов. Sell throughout процесса, не только на offer stage. Персонализируй pitch: money matters, но mission, growth, team — часто решающие факторы. Exploding offers работают против вас — дают время подумать.

---

## Теоретические основы

### Теория компенсации и Total Rewards

> **Определение:** Total Rewards — совокупная ценность, получаемая сотрудником от работодателя, включающая: compensation (base + variable), benefits (healthcare, retirement), work-life balance, career development и recognition.

WorldatWork (профессиональная ассоциация HR) формализовала модель Total Rewards в 2000-х годах, показав, что monetary compensation — лишь один из пяти компонентов привлечения и удержания талантов.

| Компонент Total Rewards | Примеры | Влияние на decision |
|------------------------|---------|-------------------|
| **Compensation** | Base salary, RSU/options, bonus | High (hygiene factor) |
| **Benefits** | Healthcare, 401k, PTO | Medium (table stakes) |
| **Work-Life** | Flexibility, remote, PTO policy | High (для senior) |
| **Development** | Career growth, learning budget | High (для IC/early career) |
| **Recognition** | Title, scope, impact | High (для senior/staff) |

### Теория переговоров

Roger Fisher и William Ury в *"Getting to Yes: Negotiating Agreement Without Giving In"* (1981) заложили основу **principled negotiation** (interest-based bargaining):

> **Principled Negotiation** (Fisher & Ury, 1981) — подход к переговорам, при котором стороны фокусируются на интересах (interests), а не позициях (positions). В контексте офферов: не «сколько вы хотите?», а «что для вас важно в работе?».

Frederick Herzberg (*"One More Time: How Do You Motivate Employees?"*, Harvard Business Review, 1968) показал различие между hygiene factors (зарплата, условия — их отсутствие демотивирует, но наличие не мотивирует) и motivators (achievement, recognition, growth). Для closing инженеров это означает: конкурентная compensation — необходимое условие, но mission, team и [[motivation|growth opportunities]] — часто решающие факторы.

## Зачем это нужно?

### Типичная ситуация

Отличный кандидат прошёл интервью. Отправили оффер по email. Тишина. Через неделю — отказ, пошёл к конкуренту. Или: "У меня другой оффер на 20% больше". Паника. Или принял, но через месяц ушёл — "не то, что ожидал".

**Без системного closing:**
- Lose candidates на финишной прямой
- Reactive negotiation
- Bad first impression
- Early turnover

**С правильным подходом:**
- Higher acceptance rate
- Win-win negotiations
- Excited new hires
- Better retention

### Статистика

| Метрика | Значение | Источник |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Candidates who negotiate | 70% | Glassdoor |
| Women who negotiate | 46% vs 52% men | PayScale |
| Candidates lost due to slow offers | 57% | Robert Half |

```
UNDERSTAND THEIR PRIORITIES:

WHAT THEY MIGHT VALUE:
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ MONEY-FOCUSED                                          │
│ • Higher base salary                                   │
│ • Sign-on bonus                                        │
│ • More equity                                          │
│ • Earlier review cycle                                 │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ CAREER-FOCUSED                                         │
│ • Title/level                                          │
│ • Scope of role                                        │
│ • Team/manager                                         │
│ • Growth opportunities                                 │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ LIFE-FOCUSED                                           │
│ • Remote/hybrid flexibility                            │
│ • Start date                                           │
│ • PTO                                                  │
│ • Parental leave                                       │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ SECURITY-FOCUSED                                       │
│ • Job stability                                        │
│ • Company trajectory                                   │
│ • Severance terms                                      │
│ • Visa sponsorship                                     │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

NEGOTIATION LEVERS BY FLEXIBILITY:

HIGH FLEXIBILITY:
• Sign-on bonus (one-time cost)
• Start date
• Title (if doesn't break bands)
• Remote/hybrid arrangement
• Equipment/stipends

MEDIUM FLEXIBILITY:
• Equity (often more room than base)
• Accelerated review
• Scope/projects
• Team assignment

LOW FLEXIBILITY:
• Base salary (bands are real)
• Level (requires calibration)
• Benefits (usually standard)
```

## Пошаговый процесс

### Pre-Offer Conversation

**When:** After positive debrief, before formal offer

```
SCRIPT:
"[Name], great news — the team was very
impressed and we'd like to move forward
with an offer.

Before I put together the details, I want
to make sure we're aligned:

1. What are you looking for in terms of
   compensation? Any specific numbers in mind?

2. Are you considering other opportunities?
   Where are you in those processes?

3. What's your ideal start date?

4. Is there anything about the role or
   company you're still uncertain about?"

PURPOSE:
• Avoid wasting time on misaligned offer
• Surface competing offers early
• Address concerns before offer
• Show you care about their needs
```

### Verbal Offer Call

**When:** Before sending written offer

```
STRUCTURE (15-20 min):

1. CONGRATULATIONS [2 min]
"[Name], I'm excited to share that we'd
like to offer you the [Role] position!

The team was really impressed with [specific
feedback]. We think you'd be a great addition."

2. WALK THROUGH OFFER [5 min]
"Let me walk you through the details:

• Base salary: $X
• Equity: X shares vesting over 4 years
• Sign-on bonus: $X
• Annual bonus target: X%

Total first-year comp is approximately $X.

Benefits include [highlights].
Start date we're thinking is [date]."

3. GAUGE REACTION [5 min]
"What's your initial reaction?"
[LISTEN]

"Does this align with what you were hoping for?"
[LISTEN]

"What questions do you have?"
[LISTEN]

4. NEXT STEPS [3 min]
"I'll send the written offer today.
Take some time to review — how does [date]
work as a deadline to get back to us?

Is there anyone else you'd like to speak
with before deciding? Happy to arrange."

5. CLOSE
"We're really excited about the possibility
of you joining. What else can I do to help
you make this decision?"
```

### Handling Counter-Offers

```
WHEN THEY ASK FOR MORE:

STEP 1: ACKNOWLEDGE
"I appreciate you sharing that. Let me
make sure I understand what's important to you."

STEP 2: CLARIFY
"Is the base salary the primary concern,
or are there other aspects of the offer?"

STEP 3: UNDERSTAND CONTEXT
"Can you help me understand what would make
this a clear yes for you?"

"Is there a competing offer? Can you share
details so I can see what we can do?"

STEP 4: RESPOND
Option A — Can accommodate:
"Let me see what I can do. I'll get back
to you by [time]."

Option B — Cannot accommodate fully:
"I understand. Here's what I CAN do:
[alternative — sign-on, equity, etc.]
Would that work?"

Option C — Cannot accommodate:
"I wish I could. Unfortunately, this is
the best we can offer for this level.
Here's what I can share about growth path
and future compensation..."
```

### When Competing Offers

```
APPROACH:

1. GET DETAILS
"Can you share the other offer details?
It helps me understand what we're competing with."

2. COMPARE APPLES-TO-APPLES
• Total comp, not just base
• Equity value (conservative)
• Benefits value
• Growth trajectory

3. SELL YOUR STRENGTHS
Focus on what differentiates you:
• Mission/impact
• Team quality
• Growth opportunity
• Culture/flexibility
• Stability/trajectory

4. DON'T GET INTO BIDDING WAR
"We don't want to get into a back-and-forth
bidding war. Here's our best and final:
[offer]. We hope you'll choose us for reasons
beyond just the number."
```

### Counter-Offer Response (When Can't Match)

```
"Thanks for being transparent about your
other offer. I appreciate that.

I want to be honest — we can't match that
base salary. Here's why:

Our compensation philosophy ties to levels,
and [base] is at the top of our band for
this level.

But here's what I CAN do:
• Sign-on bonus of $X to bridge the gap
  in year one
• Accelerated review at 6 months for
  potential level adjustment
• Additional [X] equity shares

That brings total first-year comp to [amount].

More importantly, here's why I think we're
the right choice:
[Mission/growth/team pitch]

What matters most to you in making this decision?"
```

## Распространённые ошибки

### Ошибка 1: Offer by Email Only

**Как выглядит:**
Send offer letter. Wait. Hope they accept.

**Почему это проблема:**
- No chance to gauge reaction
- Can't address concerns
- Feels transactional
- Easy to decline via email

**Как исправить:**
```
ALWAYS:
1. Phone call first (verbal offer)
2. Written offer after call
3. Follow-up call if questions
```

### Ошибка 2: Exploding Offers

**Как выглядит:**
"We need an answer by Friday" (2 days notice).

**Почему это проблема:**
- Feels coercive
- Damages employer brand
- Best candidates have options — they decline
- Accepted under pressure → early regret

**Как исправить:**
```
REASONABLE DEADLINE:
• 5-7 business days standard
• Extend if they're completing other interviews
• "We'd love to know by [date]. Let me know
  if you need more time."
```

### Ошибка 3: Only Selling Money

**Как выглядит:**
Focus entirely on compensation. Ignore mission, team, growth.

**Почему это проблема:**
- Competing on price alone = lose to higher bidder
- Miss non-monetary motivators
- Not sustainable differentiation

**Как исправить:**
```
SELL THE WHOLE PACKAGE:
• Mission and impact
• Team quality ("who you'll work with")
• Growth opportunity
• Culture and flexibility
• Interesting problems
• Company trajectory

Then: competitive compensation
```

### Ошибка 4: No Flexibility

**Как выглядит:**
"Take it or leave it. That's the offer."

**Почему это проблема:**
- Feels rigid
- Misses creative solutions
- Candidates feel undervalued

**Как исправить:**
```
ALWAYS HAVE LEVERS:
• Sign-on bonus
• Start date
• Remote flexibility
• Title within reason
• Extra equity
• Accelerated review
```

## Когда применять разные тактики

### For High-Priority Candidates

```
EXTRA EFFORT:
• Hiring manager call
• Founder/CEO touch
• Team dinner/virtual coffee
• Expedited process
• Top of range offer
• Extra sign-on
```

### For Budget-Constrained Offers

```
COMPENSATE WITH:
• Equity (cheaper for company)
• Growth story
• Flexibility (remote, hours)
• Mission/impact
• Title
• Early review promise
```

### For Competing Offer Situations

```
SPEED IS KEY:
• Same-day turnaround on decisions
• Compress approval process
• Don't make them wait
• Match deadline of other offer
```

## Связь с другими темами

**[[hiring-engineers]]** — Making offers — финальный этап hiring pipeline, описанного в hiring-engineers. Оффер должен отражать тот же уровень тщательности и персонализации, что и весь процесс найма. Если hiring philosophy строится на "hire for trajectory", то и оффер должен показывать trajectory: не только текущую compensation, но и growth opportunity, career path и learning environment.

**[[interview-process-design]]** — Качество interview process напрямую влияет на offer stage: если candidate experience была позитивной, acceptance rate выше. Информация, собранная на интервью (мотивация, career goals, другие offers), должна использоваться для персонализации оффера. Structured debrief после интервью — это также момент, когда формируется level/compensation recommendation.

**[[onboarding]]** — Оффер — это обещание, а onboarding — его выполнение. Если оффер обещает определённую роль, culture и growth opportunities, onboarding должен это deliver. Разрыв между обещаниями на offer stage и реальностью на onboarding — одна из главных причин early turnover (30% hires уходят в первые 90 дней).

**[[team-culture]]** — Культура команды — один из ключевых selling points при making offers, особенно для senior кандидатов. Money matters, но mission, team quality и growth environment часто являются решающими факторами. Менеджер, который может authentically sell culture (не hype, а реальность), имеет значительное преимущество в closing кандидатов.

## Источники и дальнейшее чтение

- **Camille Fournier, "The Manager's Path" (2017)** — Описывает роль менеджера в offer process, включая как определить правильный level, как negotiate fair compensation и как onboard успешно. Практические рекомендации по closing кандидатов без overselling.
- **Simon Sinek, "Start with Why" (2009)** — Концепция "Golden Circle" (Why → How → What) напрямую применима к making offers: кандидаты принимают офферы не только из-за compensation (what), но и из-за mission (why) и culture (how). Менеджер, который может articulate "why" компании, закрывает больше кандидатов.
- **Peter Drucker, "The Effective Executive" (2006)** — Drucker подчёркивает, что staffing decisions — самые важные решения executive, и они включают не только выбор правильного кандидата, но и правильное positioning роли и expectations. Его принципы помогают структурировать offer conversation.

## Проверь себя

> [!question]- Почему 70% кандидатов negotiate и как к этому готовиться?
> Negotiation -- норма рынка, не агрессия. Подготовка: заранее определить range (min/target/max), understand motivations кандидата (деньги vs growth vs mission), иметь non-monetary levers (remote, equity, title, PTO). Sell throughout процесса, не только на offer stage.

> [!question]- Почему exploding offers работают против компании?
> Давление на принятие решения создаёт negative experience, кандидат может принять и потом rescind, плохой employer brand через word of mouth. Лучше: дать reasonable time (5-7 дней), быть доступным для вопросов, продолжать sell.

> [!question]- Какие non-monetary факторы часто решающие при выборе оффера?
> Mission и impact (работа с meaning), team и manager (people matter most), growth opportunities (learning curve), work-life balance (remote, flexibility), technology stack и challenges. Money matters, но часто это не #1 factor для senior кандидатов.

## Ключевые карточки

Из чего состоит Total Compensation (TC)?
?
Base salary (cash), equity/stock (RSU/options), signing bonus, annual bonus, benefits (health, PTO, retirement). Для сравнения офферов нужно считать TC за 4 года включая vesting schedule. TC = Base + Equity/4yr + Bonus.

Как структурировать offer conversation?
?
1) Express excitement ("мы хотим видеть тебя в команде"), 2) Present offer clearly (все компоненты), 3) Explain rationale (как определили уровень), 4) Ask about concerns, 5) Give time to decide, 6) Follow up с enthusiasm, не давлением.

Когда и как negotiating counter-offer?
?
Слушать конкретные concerns (base vs equity vs title), иметь pre-approved flexibility, фокусироваться на total value (не только cash), использовать non-monetary levers. Помнить: цель -- win-win, не "победить" в negotiation.
