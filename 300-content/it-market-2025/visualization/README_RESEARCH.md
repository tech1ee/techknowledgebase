1. FlexJobs: 11-17% response rate
2. Remote.co: 12-18% response rate
3. Wellfound: 15-25% for engineering roles
4. WeWorkRemotely: 8-12% response rate
5. LinkedIn (personalized): 9.36% response rate

### Platform Acceptance by Region
- Russia/Belarus: Blocked (Upwork, Fiverr, Wise, Payoneer)
- Kazakhstan/Kyrgyzstan/Uzbekistan: Full access to most platforms
- Relocation (Georgia/Armenia): Complete access, no restrictions

### Outsourcing Company Hiring Status
- EPAM: Closed Russia (2022), minimal Belarus
- DataArt: Ceased all Russia/Belarus operations

- Senior (5+y): $100-250+/hour

### Timeline to First Income
- Junior: 8-12 weeks (150+ applications)
- Mid-level: 4-6 weeks (100 applications + networking)
- Senior: 2-4 weeks (targeted approach)

### Best Relocation Options
1. Georgia: 10 days, $0 visa (RECOMMENDED)
2. Kazakhstan: 10-15 days, ~$200
3. Armenia: 45-55 days, $240 (citizenship pathway)

## Critical Warnings

### BLOCKED PLATFORMS (Don't waste time)
- Upwork: Suspended May 2022, still suspended
- Fiverr: Requires VPN + workarounds (risky)
- AWS: License restrictions
- JetBrains IDEs: License restrictions

### PAYMENT METHODS BLOCKED
- Wise: Closed Russia/Belarus May 2022
- PayPal: Blocked for Russia/Belarus
- Bank transfers: Difficult Russia/Belarus (impossible some banks)

### RED FLAGS TO AVOID
- "Cash only" payments → Scam
- Extremely low rates ($10/hr for skilled work) → Scam
- No company website/LinkedIn → Not legitimate
- Asking for visa before contract → Red flag
