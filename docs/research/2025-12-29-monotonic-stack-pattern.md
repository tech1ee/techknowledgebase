# Research Report: Monotonic Stack Pattern (2024-2025)

**Date:** 2025-12-29
**Sources:** 20+

## Executive Summary

Monotonic stack solves "next greater/smaller element" problems in O(n). Four types: strictly increasing, non-decreasing, strictly decreasing, non-increasing.

## Key Insights

> "Monotonic stacks transform O(n²) nested-loop solutions into elegant O(n) single-pass algorithms." — [LeetCopilot](https://leetcopilot.dev/leetcode-pattern/monotonic-stack-queue/guide)

> "For Next Greater use Decreasing Stack. For Next Smaller use Increasing Stack." — [LeetCode Discuss](https://leetcode.com/discuss/post/2347639/a-comprehensive-guide-and-template-for-m-irii/)
