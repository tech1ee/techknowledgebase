# Research Report: Tech Salary Negotiation 2025

**Date:** 2025-12-26
**Sources Evaluated:** 22
**Research Depth:** Deep (20+ sources)

## Executive Summary

Tech salary negotiation в 2025 — обязательный навык. Разница между принятием первого оффера и стратегическим negotiation может составить $50,000+ в год или $2M+ за карьеру. Competing offers — главный рычаг, но даже без них можно добиться 10-30% increase. RSUs и signing bonus наиболее negotiable в FAANG. Ключевые ошибки: не negotiating вообще, раскрытие ожиданий слишком рано, фокус только на base salary.

## Key Findings

### 1. Negotiation Expected Everywhere
Все компании ожидают negotiation, даже на entry-level в Google/FAANG [1][2]. Не negotiating — самая большая ошибка.

### 2. Total Compensation Structure (FAANG)
- **Base Salary**: Наименее flexible, узкие bands
- **RSUs/Equity**: Наиболее negotiable, основной рычаг для increase [3]
- **Signing Bonus**: Часто не включён в initial offer намеренно [3][4]
- **Annual Bonus**: Не negotiable, зависит от performance

### 3. Competing Offers = Best Leverage
150+ non-FAANG компаний могут compete с FAANG (NVIDIA, Uber, Lyft, Pinterest). Использование их офферов как leverage — самая эффективная стратегия [1][2].

### 4. Typical Increases
- С competing offer: 15-30% increase
- Без competing offer: 5-15% increase
- Signing bonus: $10K-$100K negotiable [3]
- RSU increase: часто major source of increase [4]

## Detailed Analysis

### Compensation Components

| Component | Negotiability | Typical Range | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|-------|
| Base Salary | Low-Medium | +5-10% | Narrow bands, requires approval |
| RSUs | High | +20-50% | Major lever at senior levels |
| Signing Bonus | High | $10K-$100K | Often missing from initial offer |
| Annual Bonus | None | Set by policy | Target % fixed by level |
| Relocation | Medium | $7K-$50K | International can be higher |

### What NOT to Do

1. **Accept immediately** — оставляешь деньги на столе [5]
2. **Share salary expectations early** — ослабляет position [6]
3. **Focus only on base** — equity и bonus более flexible [2]
4. **Use ultimatums** — портит relationship [6]
5. **Negotiate over email** — phone более effective [1]
6. **Bluff about competing offers** — recruiters immediately recognize [1]
7. **Negotiate after signing** — too late [6]

### Negotiation Script Templates

**Deflecting early salary questions:**
"I'm certain we'll be able to come to a good agreement if there's a mutual fit, but it's really too early to tell."

**Responding to initial offer:**
"Really excited about the team! Thank you for the offer. I'll need time to review this, can we speak in a few days?"

**Follow-up after negotiation call:**
"Incredibly excited about the role. You mentioned looking into additional equity and signing bonus. Would love to reach $[target] total comp. I have other interviews next week but would prefer to commit here if we can hit this number."

### Remote/Relocation Specifics

- 40% employees successfully negotiate relocation [7]
- Average relocation cost: $33K (renters), $85K (homeowners) [7]
- International lump sum: up to $50K [7]
- Visa/immigration support типично включён [7]

## Recommendations

### Before Negotiation
1. Research compensation on Levels.fyi (не Glassdoor для tech)
2. Know your target total comp number
3. Get competing offers если возможно
4. Calculate value of equity realistically

### During Negotiation
1. Express enthusiasm first
2. Delay discussions до mutual fit confirmation
3. Ask for 1-2 weeks to evaluate
4. Negotiate over phone, not email
5. Focus on total comp, shift components if needed

### Order of Flexibility (easiest to hardest)
1. Signing Bonus
2. RSUs/Equity
3. Base Salary
