# Research Report: Mock Interview Guide (2024-2025)

**Date:** 2025-12-29
**Sources:** 20+

## Executive Summary

Mock interviews критически важны для подготовки. Цель: симуляция давления реального интервью. Рекомендуется 1 mock в неделю. Ключевые навыки: think aloud, whiteboard coding, STAR для behavioral. Платформы: Pramp (бесплатно), Interviewing.io, Exponent.

## Key Insights

> "In whiteboard interviews, you solve coding challenges while explaining your thought process. Focus on communicating clearly and writing legible code." — [Total Career Solutions](https://www.totalcareersolutions.com/whiteboard-coding-interview-strategies/)

> "Mock interviews help you get used to speaking out loud while solving problems, which is an essential skill in a technical interview." — [Design Gurus](https://www.designgurus.io/blog/mock-interviews-for-software-engineers)

> "Aim for at least one mock interview per week when you're actively preparing." — [Design Gurus](https://www.designgurus.io/blog/mock-interviews-for-software-engineers)

## Whiteboard Coding

> "Writing code by hand builds a different part of your brain than typing on a computer. It forces you to really understand what every line is doing." — Senior SWE at Stripe

- Пиши разборчиво
- Начни с примера/test case
- Используй псевдокод если нужно
- Оставляй место для правок

## Communication Framework

> "Structure your responses logically, beginning with an overview of your approach, followed by a step-by-step explanation." — [Design Gurus](https://www.designgurus.io/blog/coding-interviews-guide-2025)

1. Повтори задачу своими словами
2. Уточни constraints и edge cases
3. Предложи подход (brute force → optimal)
4. Обсуди trade-offs
5. Кодируй с комментариями
6. Тестируй на примерах

## STAR Method (Behavioral)

> "Behavioral interviews evaluate soft skills, teamwork, adaptability, and culture fit. They often follow the STAR format." — [Design Gurus](https://www.designgurus.io/blog/the-roadmap-to-clearing-technical-interview-in-2024)

- **S**ituation — контекст
- **T**ask — твоя задача
- **A**ction — что сделал
- **R**esult — результат (с метриками)
