# Research Report: LeetCode Roadmap & Study Plans (2024-2025)

**Date:** 2025-12-29
**Sources:** 20+

## Executive Summary

Three main study lists dominate interview prep: **Blind 75** (essentials, 4-6 weeks), **NeetCode 150** (comprehensive, 8+ weeks), **LeetCode Top 150** (official). Pattern-based learning более эффективен чем random grinding. Quality > Quantity: лучше глубоко понять 75 задач, чем поверхностно пройти 300.

## Key Insights

> "Choose Blind 75 if you have 4-6 weeks. Choose NeetCode 150 if you have 8+ weeks and targeting top companies." — [Diligentify](https://interview-whisper.com/blog/technical/leetcode-strategy-blind-75-vs-neetcode-150-2026)

> "Never watch the video explanation until you have struggled with the problem for at least 20 minutes. That pain is where the neural connections form." — [LeetCopilot](https://leetcopilot.dev/blog/neetcode-150-review-is-it-enough)

> "It's better to deeply understand 75 problems than to rush through 300." — [Tech Interview Handbook](https://www.techinterviewhandbook.org/best-practice-questions/)

## UMPIRE Method

> "The UMPIRE Method helps frame how to approach technical interview questions: Understand, Match, Plan, Implement, Review, Evaluate." — [CodePath](https://guides.codepath.com/compsci/UMPIRE-Interview-Strategy)

1. **U**nderstand — задавай вопросы, уточняй edge cases
2. **M**atch — определи паттерн (Two Pointers, DP, etc.)
3. **P**lan — напиши псевдокод
4. **I**mplement — напиши код
5. **R**eview — проверь на ошибки, edge cases
6. **E**valuate — оцени time/space complexity

## Spaced Repetition

> "Solve the problem today. Solve it again in 3 days. Solve it again in 2 weeks. If you can't solve it in 2 weeks without peeking, you never learned it." — [LeetCopilot](https://leetcopilot.dev/blog/neetcode-150-review-is-it-enough)
