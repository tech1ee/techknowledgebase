# Networking Tactics: как строить relationships в tech

85% вакансий находят через networking. Один referral = 40 cold applications. Developers who network видят 18% рост зарплаты vs 3% без networking. Но networking — не о том, чтобы собирать визитки. Это о строительстве genuine relationships, которые работают в обе стороны.

---

## Терминология

| Термин | Что это |
|--------|---------|
| **Informational interview** | Разговор для обмена опытом, не о работе |
| **Warm intro** | Знакомство через общего контакта |
| **Cold outreach** | Обращение к незнакомому челове|
| **Value-first** | Сначала дать ценность, потом просить |

---

## Для интровертов: это работает по-другому

```
МИФ: Networking = много людей, много small talk
РЕАЛЬНОСТЬ: Качество > количество

Преимущества интроверта:
• Глубокое слушание → запоминаешь детали
• Думаешь перед словами → качественный input
• Предпочитаешь 1:1 → deeper relationships
• Content creation → passive networking

"Two or three meaningful connections serve you better
than dozens of superficial ones."

ФОРМАТ ДЛЯ ИНТРОВЕРТА:
• Online communities (Slack, Discord) — async, thoughtful
• Written content → reach thousands without talking
• 1:1 coffee chats вместо meetups
• Conference volunteering (structured, роль задана)
```

## Multi-Channel Strategy

### 1. LinkedIn (обязательно)

```
DAILY (5-10 мин):
• React на 3-5 постов в feed
• Один thoughtful comment в день
• Accept relevant connection requests

WEEKLY:
• 1 пост (technical insight, learning, opinion)
• 5-10 connection requests к target people
• 1-2 DM conversations

MONTHLY:
• Update profile с новыми достижениями
• Review и accept pending requests

CONTENT IDEAS:
✓ Решение технической проблемы
✓ Lessons learned из проекта
✓ Мнение о тренде (Compose, KMP, AI)
✓ Tool/library recommendation
✓ Career reflection

✗ Generic motivation
```

## Informational Interviews

### Что это

```
15-30 минутный разговор для:
• Узнать об опыте в компании/роли
• Понять культуру и challenges
• Получить advice
• Построить relationship

ЧТО ЭТО НЕ:
• Просьба о работе
• Pitch себя
• Sales call
```

### Как попросить

```
Subject: Quick question about Android work at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I'm [brief intro]. I noticed your work on [specific project/post] —
really impressive approach to [specific thing].

I'm exploring [career direction] and would love to hear about
your experience at [Company], especially regarding [specific topic].

Would you have 15 minutes for a quick call? Happy to work around
your schedule.

Best,
[Your Name]

---

RESPONSE RATE BOOSTERS:
• Mention specific work (shows research)
• Keep it short (busy people)
• Make it easy ("15 min, any time")
• Don't ask for job
```

### Вопросы для разговора

```
О работе:
• What's a typical day/week like for you?
• What are the biggest challenges your team faces?
• What skills are most valuable in your role?

О компании:
• How would you describe the engineering culture?
• What's the balance between shipping and quality?
• How does the company approach [remote/growth/etc]?

Career advice:
• What would you recommend for someone transitioning to [X]?
• What do you wish you knew earlier in your career?
• Any resources that were particularly helpful?

ЗАКАНЧИВАЙ:
"Thank you so much for your time. Is there anyone else
you'd recommend I speak with?"
(→ warm intro to next person)
```

### Follow-up

```
В течение 24 часов:

Subject: Thank you!

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to chat today. Your insight about
[specific point] was really valuable — I hadn't considered [thing].

I'll definitely [action based on their advice].

Looking forward to staying in touch. Let me know if I can ever
be helpful!

Best,
[Your Name]

---

ПОДДЕРЖАНИЕ СВЯЗИ:
• Share relevant article/resource через месяц
• React на их LinkedIn posts
• Send holiday greeting
• Update когда их advice helped
```

## Building Relationships (не transactions)

### Value-First Approach

```
НЕПРАВИЛЬНО:
Day 1: Connect
Day 2: "Do you have any openings?"
Day 3: *crickets*

ПРАВИЛЬНО:
Week 1: Connect + genuine comment on their work
Week 2: Share article relevant to their interest
Week 4: Another engagement on their content
Week 6: Offer help ("Saw you're looking for X, I know someone")
Week 8: Informational interview request
Week 12: Relationship built → referral natural

КЛЮЧЕВОЕ: 6-8 touchpoints до ask.
```

### Giving Before Asking

```
ЧЕМ МОЖЕШЬ ПОМОЧЬ:

Знания:
• Answer their questions в communities
• Share useful resources
• Technical advice

Connections:
• Intro к людям которых знаешь
• "I know someone who might help with [X]"

Visibility:
• Share их content
• Recommend their work
• Leave reviews/testimonials

"The best networkers are connectors who ask
'How can I help?' before 'What can I get?'"
```

## Cold Outreach Templates

### LinkedIn Connection Request

```
Hi [Name], I'm a Senior Android Developer interested in [Company]'s
work on [specific project]. Would love to connect and learn more
about your team's approach to [topic].
```

### Email to Unknown Contact

```
Subject: Fellow Android dev — quick question about [topic]

Hi [Name],

I came across your talk at [Conference] about [topic]. Your approach
to [specific thing] was really interesting.

I'm working on something similar at [My Company] and have a quick
question: [specific, easy-to-answer question].

No worries if you're too busy — just thought I'd reach out.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### After Meeting at Event

```
Subject: Great meeting you at [Event]

Hi [Name],

It was great chatting about [specific topic] at [Event] yesterday.
Your point about [specific insight] stuck with me.

I'd love to continue the conversation. Coffee sometime (virtual works)?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Mistakes to Avoid

```
❌ "Great post!" comments
   → Add insight: "Great post! This reminds me of [X]..."

❌ Asking for job in first message
   → Build relationship first (6-8 touches)

❌ Connecting без context
   → Always add note why connecting

❌ Only reaching out when you need something
   → Regular engagement even when not job hunting

❌ Mass copy-paste outreach
   → Personalize each message (they can tell)

❌ Not following up
   → Always send thank you within 24h

❌ Talking only about yourself
   → Ask questions, listen, show genuine interest
```
