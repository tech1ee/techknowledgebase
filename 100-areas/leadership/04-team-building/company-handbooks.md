# Справочники компаний

> **TL;DR:** Handbooks — не HR документы, а living documentation культуры и процессов. GitLab: 3000+ страниц public. Valve: знаменитый handbook для flat structure. Basecamp: calm company philosophy. Изучай лучшие примеры, адаптируй под себя.

---

## Famous Handbooks

### GitLab Handbook

```
URL: https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/

WHAT'S INSIDE:
• 3000+ pages
• Everything public
• How decisions made
• Salary calculator
• All processes

KEY SECTIONS:
• Values
• Communication
• Working remotely
• Engineering practices
• People policies

WHY IT'S GREAT:
• Radical transparency
• Living document
• Scales remote culture
• Reduces meetings
```

### Valve Handbook

```
URL: google "Valve Employee Handbook PDF"

WHAT'S INSIDE:
• Flat structure explained
• How to choose projects
• Peer review system
• Company philosophy

KEY QUOTES:
"Hierarchy is great for maintaining
predictability and repeatability.
It simplifies planning and makes it
easier to control large groups.

But it's not great for creativity."

WHY IT'S FAMOUS:
• No managers model
• Controversial but influential
• Honest about trade-offs
```

### Basecamp Handbook

```
URL: https://github.com/basecamp/handbook

WHAT'S INSIDE:
• Company philosophy
• Benefits
• Work-life balance
• Making decisions

KEY PRINCIPLES:
• Calm company
• 40-hour weeks
• No hustle culture
• Profit > growth

WHY IT'S VALUABLE:
• Counter-cultural
• Explicit trade-offs
• Practical details
```

## Handbook Structure Template

```markdown
# [Company] Handbook

## 1. Who We Are
- Mission
- Values (with behaviors)
- History
- Culture principles

## 2. How We Work
- Communication norms
- Meeting culture
- Decision making
- Remote/hybrid policy

## 3. Engineering Practices
- Development process
- Code review
- Testing standards
- On-call/incidents

## 4. People
- Hiring process
- Onboarding
- Performance reviews
- Career growth
- Compensation philosophy

## 5. Benefits & Policies
- PTO policy
- Parental leave
- Learning budget
- Equipment

## 6. Teams & Structure
- Org chart
- Team descriptions
- Roles & responsibilities

## 7. Operations
- Tools we use
- Security practices
- Expense policy

---
Last updated: [Date]
Questions: [Contact]
```

## How to Use These

```
1. READ for inspiration
   Not copy-paste. Understand principles.

2. ADAPT to your context
   Startup ≠ Enterprise
   Your culture ≠ their culture

3. START SMALL
   Begin with values + key processes
   Expand over time

4. MAKE IT LIVING
   Update regularly
   Team can contribute
   Version control (Git)

5. REFERENCE OFTEN
   Onboarding
   Decisions
   Conflict resolution
```
